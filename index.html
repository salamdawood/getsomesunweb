Track Your Daily Sunlight — Get Some Sun

Get Some Sun helps you spend healthier time outdoors. Log your sunlight minutes, see progress rings and streaks, and learn when it’s safest to step outside — all in one place. Stay mindful of your daylight, boost your mood, and let the sun work for you.

Core Features

  • Daily Sun Tracking — log your sunlight time in seconds
  • Progress Ring + Streaks for daily motivation
  • Smart UV Awareness — live UV index with color-coded tips
  • HealthKit Integration — syncs “Time in Daylight” automatically
  • Dynamic Light/Dark Themes — responsive to your system mode

Why You’ll Love It

  • Private by design — all data stays on your device
  • Gentle daily reminders to get outside
  • Animated mascot “Sonny” reacts to your progress
  • Simple visuals and Apple-style layout built for iOS 18

Privacy

  • No sign-ups or cloud accounts — data stays local
  • Uses WeatherKit securely for live UV data
  • Health access is optional and fully controlled by you
