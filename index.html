Get Some Sun

Build a healthier daily sunlight habit with simple tracking, Apple Health integration, live UV guidance, and personalized reminders.

Whether you're trying to improve your routine, spend more time outdoors, or simply stay consistent, Get Some Sun helps you make sunlight part of your day.

Download on the App Store

Features

  • Track your daily sunlight in seconds
  • Automatically import Time in Daylight from Apple Health
  • Live UV Index powered by WeatherKit
  • Sun Plan with personalized daily UV guidance
  • Daily progress ring and streak tracking
  • Friendly reminders to help you stay consistent
  • Animated companion Sonny celebrates your progress
  • Supports both Light Mode and Dark Mode

Get Some Sun Pro

  • Best Sun Window recommendations
  • UV Peak insights
  • Hourly UV Timeline
  • Custom daily reminder time

Privacy

  • No accounts or sign-ups required
  • Sunlight logs and settings remain on your device
  • Apple Health access is completely optional
  • Location is only used to retrieve local UV information
  • Privacy-friendly anonymous analytics help improve the app
